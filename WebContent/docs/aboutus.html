We simplify your life by off loading the heavy duty task of remembering things to do in day to day life so that you may focus on your loved ones and on your business.

"Remind me on" is a platform that enables you remember and do things when they are scheduled and never forget anything from this point. We simplifies the way you create reminders for yourself and how you get notifications. When you schedule a reminder you get an email on the due time to remind you about the task. You may also receive a phone call and SMS if you opt for that. The phone call will inform you about the task in English language.
